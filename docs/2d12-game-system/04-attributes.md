# Attributes

## Overview

Characters are defined by eight core attributes divided into Physical and Mental categories. Each attribute is rated from 0 to 7.

## Physical Attributes

- Strength (STR): Raw physical power for lifting, striking, and feats of might.
- Constitution (CON): Endurance, resistance to harm, and physical toughness.
- Dexterity (DEX): Precision, speed, and hand-eye coordination.
- Agility (AGI): Reflexes, balance, and body control.

## Mental Attributes

- Reason (REA): Logic, memory, and problem-solving.
- Insight (INS): Awareness, perception, and intuition.
- Charisma (CHA): Social presence, charm, and leadership.
- Willpower (WIL): Mental fortitude, determination, and force of will.
