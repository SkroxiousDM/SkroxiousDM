## Chapter 4: Traits

Traits are the defining features and abilities that distinguish a hero from the common run of humanity. Many Traits require certain Skill Ranks, Attribute scores, or other Traits before they can be learned.

Traits provide both **active** and **passive** benefits. Passive benefits persistently modify Attributes, Skills, or other game elements. Many Traits also grant access to **Actions**, **Reactions**, and **Desperate Actions** (see pages 29–30).

***

## Combat Traits
### Hound

- **Benefit:** Choose one character. You gain Advantage on all base Dexterity and Strength Checks made to reach them, so long as they are no farther than Far range. This lasts until you take a Rest or designate a new target.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on one attack against your designated target.

***

### Tough

*Prerequisite: Strength 1*

- **Benefit:** +1 Vitality.
- **Benefit:** +2 Armor while unarmored.
- **Desperate Action:** When a single attack would deal more than 1 Wound, reduce it to 1.

***

### Slayer

*Prerequisite: Strength 1 or Dexterity 1*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Fighting and Archery Skill Checks made against the Risen.
- **Reaction:** If more than two Risen make Fighting Skill Checks against you in a round, you may attack one adjacent Risen with a Fighting or Archery Skill Check.
- **Desperate Action:** Make a Fighting or Archery Skill Check against a Risen.

***

### Berserker

*Prerequisite: Strength 4*

- **Benefit:** When you would receive a Wound that kills you, make a Strength Check (CR 10). On a success, you do not die and instead heal 1 Wound. Each subsequent use of this benefit increases the CR by 5. A Rest resets the CR to 10.

***

### Dual Wielder

- **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding two swords, daggers, maces, axes, or fighting knives.
- **Benefit:** When you would inflict a Major Wound while dual wielding, inflict one additional Wound.

***

### Opportunist

*Prerequisites: Dual Wielder, Dexterity 2, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When you successfully Evade while dual wielding swords, daggers, or fighting knives, you may immediately attack your attacker with a Fighting Skill Check.

***

### Bruiser

*Prerequisites: Dual Wielder, Strength 2, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When your attack while dual wielding swords, axes, or maces is mitigated by armor, you may make an additional Fighting Skill Check against the same target.

***

### Dervish

*Prerequisites: Opportunist or Bruiser, Strength 3 or Dexterity 3*

- **Desperate Action:** While dual wielding, make a single Fighting Skill Check against a number of adjacent characters up to your Dexterity or Strength score.

***

### Ironclad

- **Benefit:** Armor requires 1 fewer Strength to wear. You may forgo this benefit to have all armor grant +1 Armor instead.
- **Benefit:** Armor's Dexterity limit is increased by 1. You may forgo this benefit to have all armor grant +1 Armor instead.

***

### Retaliation

*Prerequisites: Ironclad, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When an attack from an adjacent source is mitigated by your armor, you may immediately make a Fighting Skill Check against the attacker.

***

### Dangerous

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Fighting or Archery*

- **Desperate Action:** Make a Fighting or Archery Skill Check as though you had Rank 5. If your Fighting or Archery is already Rank 5, this attack affects two characters (make a single Skill Check).
- **Desperate Action:** You may take one additional Reaction this turn.

***

### Deadly

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding only a single one-handed weapon. This bonus is retained while also carrying a shield.
- **Benefit:** +3 Chance of Major Wounds with one-handed weapons. Holding a shield or dual wielding reduces this bonus by 1.

***

### Scrapper

*Prerequisites: Strength 1 or Dexterity 1, Rank 1 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +3 Chance of Major Wounds on unarmed Fighting Skill Checks.
- **Benefit:** When you spend Tenacity to gain Advantage on an unarmed attack, that attack inflicts Wounds as though you were armed.

***

### Formidable

*Prerequisites: Strength 1, Rank 1 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding a two-handed axe, mace, or sword.
- **Benefit:** When you would inflict a Major Wound with a two-handed weapon, inflict one additional Wound.

***

### Mighty

*Prerequisite: Strength 1*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all non-combat Strength Checks made to push, pull, or lift objects.

***

### Butcher

*Prerequisites: Mighty, Strength 2, Rank 3 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** The first time each round you inflict a Wound or Major Wound with a two-handed axe, mace, or sword, inflict one additional Wound.

***

### Cleaver

*Prerequisites: Mighty, Strength 3, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When you kill a character with a two-handed weapon, you may immediately attack a character adjacent to them.

***

### Manslayer

*Prerequisites: Cleaver, Strength 4*

- **Benefit:** When Cleaver's Reaction Skill Check exceeds CR 25, you may attack an additional character adjacent to the first.

***

### Lasher

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 1, Rank 1 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** Your base Evasion becomes 10 + your Fighting Skill Rank while wielding a spear or staff in both hands.
- **Reaction:** If you were not adjacent to any enemy on your last turn, gain Advantage on your first Fighting Skill Check this turn with a two-handed spear or staff.

***

### Staver

*Prerequisites: Lasher, Dexterity 2, Rank 3 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When you would make a Fighting Skill Check with Advantage using a two-handed spear or staff, you may instead make a single attack against two adjacent characters.

***

### Lancer

*Prerequisites: Staver, Dexterity 3, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Desperate Action:** Spend one Tenacity to make a single Fighting Skill Check with a two-handed spear or staff against any number of adjacent characters.

***

### Duelist

*Prerequisites: Mind 1, Rank 1 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** When you would make a Fighting Skill Check with Advantage, roll three d20 instead of two and take the highest result.
- **Benefit:** When adjacent to only one other character, gain +2 Chance of Major Wounds on Fighting Skill Checks.

***

### Skirmisher

*Prerequisites: Duelist, Mind 2, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +1 Chance of Major Wounds on Fighting Skill Checks.
- **Benefit:** Once per round, when you use a Reaction, you may use one Reaction you have already used this round.

***

### Charger

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +2 Chance of Major Wounds on Fighting Skill Checks if you have no current Wounds.
- **Benefit:** If you move from a non-adjacent position to attack, gain +2 Chance of Major Wounds.
- **Benefit:** You may use Strength instead of Dexterity when calculating Alacrity.

***

### Reckless

*Prerequisites: Strength 1, Rank 2 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** You may choose to give all your Fighting Skill Checks—and all attacks made against you—Advantage for the round.

***

### Dirty Fighter

*Prerequisites: Strength 1 or Dexterity 1, Rank 2 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When you fail to hit with a Fighting Skill attack, you may make an immediate second attack with Disadvantage.
- **Benefit:** You may choose to make Fighting Skill Checks with Disadvantage. When you succeed on a Fighting Skill Check made with Disadvantage, you inflict one additional Wound.

***

### Defender

*Prerequisites: Strength 2, Rank 2 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** Shields grant +1 Armor.
- **Reaction:** When you do not move on your turn, you may raise a heavy shield to gain cover for the round, imposing Disadvantage on Archery and Blooded Skill Checks made against you.

***

### Shieldbearer

*Prerequisites: Defender, Rank 3 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When your attack fails to inflict any Wound, you may immediately make an additional shield bash using your Fighting Skill.

***

### Stalwart

*Prerequisites: Shieldbearer, Strength 3, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When adjacent to more than one character who has attacked you this round, you may make an attack with your Fighting Skill.
- **Benefit:** If you end your turn adjacent to three or more hostile characters, you gain Advantage on your next Fighting Skill Check.

***

### Vigilant

*Prerequisites: Shieldbearer, Strength 3, Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Reaction:** When an adjacent character attacks a friendly character adjacent to you, you may immediately attack the attacker with your Fighting Skill.
- **Reaction:** If an adjacent ally without the Vigilant Trait would receive one or more Wounds and you are wielding a shield, you may take those Wounds instead. They are mitigated as though by armor.

***

### Savage

*Prerequisites: Strength 1, Rank 3 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +1 Chance of Major Wounds for every two Wounds you currently carry.
- **Reaction:** When you receive a Major Wound not mitigated by armor, you may immediately attack.
- **Desperate Action:** Take 1 Wound to make an additional Fighting Skill attack this turn.

***

### Agile

*Prerequisite: Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +1 Armor while unarmored.
- **Benefit:** +1 Alacrity while unarmored.
- **Benefit:** +1 Chance of Major Wounds while unarmored.
- **Reaction:** If you Wound an unwounded character, you may immediately attack them again with a Fighting Skill Check.

***

### Warrior

*Prerequisites: Rank 5 Fighting, Archery, or Blood*

- **Benefit:** +1 Chance of Major Wounds.
- **Reaction:** When a character willingly moves away from you on their turn, you may use your Reaction to attack them.
- **Desperate Action:** If you inflict a Major Wound, you may attack again immediately.

***

### Impassable

*Prerequisite: Rank 5 Fighting*

- **Benefit:** +4 Armor.
- **Special:** Your Dexterity may not increase your Evasion.

***

## Mobility Traits

### Light-Footed

*Prerequisite: Dexterity 2*

- **Benefit:** +2 Alacrity.
- **Benefit:** +1 Evasion while wielding neither weapons nor shields.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Thievery Skill for Dexterity-based Checks involving tumbling or acrobatics.

***

### Athletic

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3 or Strength 3, Light-Footed*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Dexterity-based Checks to jump, climb, and tumble.
- **Benefit:** Reduce falling damage by half as though mitigated by armor.
- **Desperate Action:** Prevent up to 2 Wounds from a single fall.

***

### Nimble

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 4, Light-Footed*

- **Desperate Action:** All attacks against you have Disadvantage for the round.
- **Desperate Action:** Halve the Wounds from one attack that hits you, as though by armor.

***

## Thievery Traits

### Thrower

- **Benefit:** You may use your Thievery Skill to throw knives, hatchets, or hand axes as attacks against Adjacent or Near characters.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Thievery Skill Checks to conceal small blades on your person.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on a Thievery Skill Check to throw a blade or small axe.

***

### Quick-Fingered

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 1, Rank 1 Thievery*

- **Benefit:** Once per Thievery Skill Check to pick pockets or perform sleight of hand, you may reroll all dice.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Thievery Checks involving coins, rings, dice, and jewelry.
- **Benefit:** If you would fail a Skill Check to win at a game of chance, you may cheat by making a Thievery Skill Check at Advantage and using that result instead.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Thievery Skill one Rank.

***

### Lockpick

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Thievery*

- **Benefit:** You may use your Thievery Skill to pick locks.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on a Thievery Skill Check to pick a lock.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Thievery Skill one Rank.

***

### Burglar

*Prerequisites: Lockpick, Mind 1, Rank 5 Thievery*

- **Benefit:** You can always improvise lock-picking tools from materials at hand, regardless of equipment carried.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Thievery Skill Checks to pick locks when you have proper picks.
- **Benefit:** Once you have opened a lock, you automatically succeed at all future Thievery Skill Checks to open it again.

***

## Stealth Traits

### Subtle

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Stealth*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Stealth Checks to blend into crowds.
- **Benefit:** Survival Skill Checks made to track you have Disadvantage.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Stealth Skill one Rank.

***

### Cutthroat

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Stealth*

- **Benefit:** Major Wounds resulting from Surprise Attacks made with Advantage inflict one additional Wound.
- **Benefit:** Your killing blows are silent.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Stealth Skill one Rank.

***

### Ambusher

*Prerequisites: Cutthroat, Rank 5 Stealth*

- **Benefit:** Wounds from Surprise Attacks against characters who have not yet acted this round reduce their Alacrity to zero for the round.
- **Benefit:** You may choose to make any of your killing blows silent.

***

### Unseen

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 3, Rank 5 Stealth*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Stealth Skill Checks if you move only to an adjacent location on your turn.
- **Benefit:** You may spend one Tenacity to reroll a Stealth Skill Check.

***

## Archery Traits

### Archer

- **Benefit:** +2 Alacrity if your bow or crossbow is readied.
- **Benefit:** +1 Evasion while wielding a bow or crossbow.
- **Benefit:** If you do not move on your turn, Major Wounds inflicted by your Archery Skill inflict one additional Wound.

***

### Crossbowman

- **Benefit:** +2 Alacrity if your crossbow is loaded.
- **Benefit:** When you make an Archery Skill Check with a crossbow, you may treat your Skill as though it were two Ranks higher, to a maximum of Rank 5.

***

### Marksman

*Prerequisites: Dexterity 1, Rank 3 Archery*

- **Benefit:** +2 Chance of Major Wounds with Archery Skill Checks.
- **Benefit:** You may make Archery Skill Checks against Distant characters without Disadvantage.
- **Benefit:** Partial cover does not impose Disadvantage on your Archery Skill Checks.

***

### Warden

*Prerequisites: Marksman, Dexterity 2, Rank 5 Archery*

- **Benefit:** +2 Evasion while wielding a bow or crossbow.
- **Benefit:** You no longer suffer Disadvantage on Archery Skill Checks made against Adjacent characters.
- **Reaction:** After attacking a Far or Distant character, you may make an additional Archery Skill Check against a Near or Adjacent character.

***

### Ranger

*Prerequisites: Marksman, Dexterity 2, Rank 5 Archery*

- **Benefit:** Archery Skill Checks made from a hidden position with bows, crossbows, or thrown weapons do not automatically reveal your location.
- **Reaction:** If you make an Archery Skill Check against a Distant character and inflict a Major Wound, you may attack a character adjacent to them.

***

## Knowledge and Social Traits

### Tongued

*Prerequisite: Mind 1*

- **Benefit:** Learn to speak, read, and write two languages you do not already know.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on a Manipulation Skill Check made in either of those languages.

***

### Traveled

*Prerequisite: Mind 1*

- **Benefit:** Increase three different Skills by one Rank each.

***

### Jack of All Trades

*Prerequisites: Traveled, Mind 4, Rank 3 in any Skill*

- **Benefit:** Increase four different Skills by one Rank each.

***

### Scholar

*Prerequisites: Jack of All Trades, Mind 5, Rank 5 in any Skill*

- **Benefit:** Increase five different Skills by one Rank each.
- **Benefit:** +1 to your lowest Attribute.

***

### Tactician

*Prerequisites: Presence 1 and Mind 1*

- **Benefit:** At any point you could make an attack, you may instead forgo it to allow a Near character to use their Reaction to attack. If you do not move, they may also move a Near distance.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Learned Skill to assess and recall information about battle tactics, military strategy, and campaign logistics.

***

### Leader

*Prerequisites: Tactician, Presence 2 or Mind 2*

- **Benefit:** When you spend Tenacity to grant Advantage to another character, you may instead grant it to up to two characters simultaneously.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage when using your Learned Skill to assess military tactics, strategy, and logistics.
- **Desperate Action:** If your Mind or Presence is 4 or greater, you may award another character Tenacity in the same manner as the Arbiter.

***

### Willful

*Prerequisite: Mind 2*

- **Benefit:** Gain 1 Vitality.
- **Benefit:** If your Mind is 4 or greater, gain 1 additional Vitality.

***

### Artisan

*Prerequisite: Mind 3*

- **Benefit:** Choose a specific craft at the time of learning this Trait. Your Learned Skill now applies to all Skill Checks involving that craft.
- **Desperate Action:** When using your Learned Skill for a crafting task, make the Skill Check with Advantage twice and take the higher result.

***

### Tutored

*Prerequisites: Mind 1, Rank 1 Learned*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Learned Skill Checks to identify nobles, lords, and persons of rank.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Learned Skill Checks to recall the laws and customs of cities or regions.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Learned Skill Checks to compose eloquent formal writing in your native language.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Learned Skill one Rank.

***

### Counterfeiter

*Prerequisites: Mind 2, Rank 1 Learned, Rank 1 Thievery*

- **Benefit:** You may use your Learned Skill to assess the authenticity of documents, coins, seals, and goods.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Thievery Skill to forge documents, coins, seals, or other goods.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Mind as the base Attribute for Thievery Skill Checks involving forgery and lock-picking.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Thievery Skill or Learned Skill one Rank.

***

### Deceiver

*Prerequisites: Counterfeiter, Mind 3*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Learned and Thievery Skill Checks involving authenticating or forging documents, coins, and goods.
- **Benefit:** If you study an object such as a key, coin, or wax seal for one full Turn, you memorize it in exact detail.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Learned Skill to replicate handwriting you have examined.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Thievery Skill or Learned Skill one Rank.

***

## Survival and Wilderness Traits

### Bestial

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Survival*

- **Benefit:** You may use your Manipulation Skill on non-human animals at Advantage.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Intimidation Skill on non-human animals at Advantage.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Insight Skill on non-human animals at Advantage.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Survival Skill one Rank.

***

### Huntsman

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Survival*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Survival Skill Checks to track characters, lay traps, skin animals, or learn about beasts.
- **Benefit:** +2 Chance of Major Wounds against beasts.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Survival Skill one Rank.

***

### Nomad

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Survival*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Strength Checks to endure extreme climates, hunger, and thirst.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on one Survival Skill Check.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Survival Skill Checks to determine location and direction.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Survival Skill one Rank.

***

### Wildling

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Survival*

- **Benefit:** You may use your Survival Skill for all Dexterity-based Checks to climb or swim.
- **Benefit:** You may use your Survival Skill in place of your Stealth Skill to hide and move silently in natural environments. If your Stealth Skill is Rank 5, gain Advantage on all such Checks.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Survival Skill one Rank.

***

### Horseman

*Prerequisite: Rank 2 Survival*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Fighting Skill Checks against unmounted characters while mounted on a trained horse.
- **Benefit:** Your trained horse gains +1 Evasion for each Rank of your Survival Skill.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Dexterity and Survival Checks to control trained mounts.

***

## Perception Traits

### Alert

*Prerequisite: Rank 4 Perception*

- **Benefit:** If your Alacrity is less than 10, treat it as 10.
- **Benefit:** Surprise attacks against you do not gain Advantage.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Perception Skill one Rank.

***

## Apothecary Traits

### Poisoner

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Apothecary*

- **Benefit:** You may spend one Tenacity to automatically succeed at a Strength Check against a poison after seeing the initial result.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Strength Checks against poison.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Apothecary Skill Checks to diagnose or identify poisons.
- **Benefit:** The CR to resist all poisons you create is increased by 2.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Apothecary Skill one Rank.

***

### Poultice Maker

*Prerequisites: Rank 1 Apothecary, Rank 1 Survival*

- **Benefit:** You may spend one Tenacity to use a raw herb as though it were properly brewed, applying its full prepared effect.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Survival Checks to locate herbs and ingredients.
- **Benefit:** You find twice the normal yield when foraging for medicinal plants.
- **Benefit:** The CR for brewing all potions and poisons you craft is reduced by 2.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Apothecary Skill one Rank.

***

### Master Alchemist

*Prerequisites: Poultice Maker or Poisoner, Rank 5 Apothecary*

- **Benefit:** You may create potions or poisons with the combined active effects of two distinct ingredients.
- **Benefit:** When you succeed at an Apothecary Skill Check to brew a potion or poison, you produce twice the normal quantity.

***

### Bonesetter

*Prerequisites: Mind 1, Rank 3 Apothecary*

- **Benefit:** Healing draughts you prepare restore one additional Minor Wound.
- **Desperate Action:** Spend one Tenacity to heal a recently slain character a Minor Wound, restoring them to life. They must have died within a number of minutes equal to your Mind.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Apothecary Skill one Rank.

***

## Presence and Intimidation Traits

### Unyielding

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Intimidation*

- **Benefit:** You may use your highest Attribute as the base for Intimidation Skill Checks.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage when contesting Manipulation and Intimidation Skill Checks.
- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on one Intimidation Skill Check.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Intimidation Skill one Rank.

***

### Performer

*Prerequisites: Presence 1, Rank 1 Performance*

- **Desperate Action:** Reroll a failed Performance Skill Check.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Performance Skill Checks made for audiences who share your language.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Performance Skill one Rank.

***

### Master Performer

*Prerequisites: Performer, Rank 5 Performance*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Manipulation Skill Checks made against any character you have successfully performed for, until they complete a Rest.

***

### Liar

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Manipulation*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on all Manipulation Skill Checks intended to deceive through deliberate falsehood.
- **Benefit:** Characters who have not previously met you have Disadvantage on their first Insight Skill Check made against you.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.

***

### Alluring

*Prerequisites: Presence 1, Rank 1 Manipulation*

- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on one Manipulation or Performance Skill Check.
- **Benefit:** You may spend one Tenacity to gain Advantage on all Manipulation Skill Checks against a single character until either of you completes a Rest.
- **Benefit:** +1 Presence.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.

***

### Mercantile

*Prerequisites: Mind 1 or Presence 1, Rank 1 Manipulation*

- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Learned Skill Checks to identify or appraise goods and objects.
- **Benefit:** Gain Advantage on Manipulation and Insight Checks for negotiating prices and arranging deals.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Manipulation Skill one Rank.

***

### Wise

*Prerequisite: Rank 1 Insight*

- **Desperate Action:** Gain Advantage on one Insight Skill Check.
- **Benefit:** If you fail an Insight Skill Check, this benefit refreshes as though you had taken a Rest.
- **Benefit:** Increase your Insight Skill one Rank.

***

- **Desperate Action:** Award one Tenacity to any number of Near characters in the same manner as the Arbiter. This Tenacity is lost when those characters complete a Rest.
